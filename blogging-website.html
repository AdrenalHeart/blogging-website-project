Dead by Daylight

The Fundamentals

Survivors

Survivors, alone, make up 50% of the game. There is a vast majority of characters, whom each have a unique set of perks, that contribute to the game's unpredictability. In every trial, the game sets 4 players against a killer. Their objective is to repair a total of 5 generators to power up the exit gates and potentially escape the trial. Thus hurdling the players into an intense, dramatic game of Chess.

Killers

Killers, just like Survivors, make up the other 50% of the game. There are many different characters to choose between. Similar to their survivor counterpart, killers have a unique set of perks and to each, their own specific ability. The killer's abilities can fit anyone's playstyle, whether it be Phasing, Cloaking, or Blinking; more on that later. The killer's objective is to prevent the survivors from repairing the generators and escaping the trial.
